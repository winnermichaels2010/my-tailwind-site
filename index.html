    Classes of Food

    There are 6 main classes of food that your body needs to stay healthy and strong:

    🍞

    Carbohydrates

    Main source of energy Examples: Rice, bread, yam, garri, potatoes

    🍗

    Proteins

    Help in body building and repair Examples: Beans, meat, fish, eggs, milk

    🧈

    Fats and Oils

    Give energy and keep the body warm Examples: Butter, oil, groundnut, avocado

    🍊

    Vitamins

    Protect the body from diseases Examples: Fruits and vegetables (orange, mango, spinach)

    🥛

    Minerals

    Help in strong bones, teeth, and blood formation Examples: Milk (calcium), meat (iron), vegetables

    💧

    Water

    Helps in digestion, circulation, and temperature control We get it from drinking water, fruits, and soups
